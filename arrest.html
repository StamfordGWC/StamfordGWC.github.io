What Your Family Should Know in Case of Arrest

  • Document of origin or false documents should not be carried on you.
    • Examples: Birth certificate, passport
  • Have a phone number of a close relative to contact when either you or your parent is arrested.
  • Know who will provide care for you, if your parents are arrested.
  • Have a trusted person know where your legal documents are and have access to them.
  • Use this Immigration and Customs Enforcement website to locate your parent. Have their immigration number handy.
