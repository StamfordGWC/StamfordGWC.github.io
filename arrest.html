In Case of Your Parent or Guardian's Arrest

Document of origin or false documents should not be carried on you.

  • Examples: Birth certificate, passport

Have a phone # of a close relative to contact when either you or your parent is arrested.

Know who will provide care for you, if your parents are arrested.

Have a legal trusted person know where your legal documents are and have access to them.

Use this website to locate your parent. Know their immigration number.
