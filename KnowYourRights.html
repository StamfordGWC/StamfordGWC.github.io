Know Your Rights

Don’t open the door unless they have a signed warrant by a judge.

If they do have a warrant ask them to slip it under the door and make sure it is signed by a judge.

If the warrant is signed by a judge, the person they are seeking should go out another door and go to them so other family members are not affected.

ICE does not have the right to search your house without a warrant.

Don’t sign any documents without a lawyer.

When ICE asks you questions simply state that you do not wish to answer their questions.
