About us

Our Goal

Each Girls Who Code club aims to use technology to benefit the community. In reading and hearing the news about DACA, we recognized our own lack of knowledge about immigration issues and saw a need to get the word out to our peers. We would like to help our peers understand how immigration issues are likely to affect each of their lives. The hope is that this information is shared with their family and friends. Often, children of immigrant parents are the source of their parents' information.

How It Might Help Others

  • Address what the current general understanding is of a non-documented person’s rights.
  • How best to protect yourself and your family.
  • Identify community resources.
  • Know your rights under the law.
