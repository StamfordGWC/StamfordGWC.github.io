About us

Our Goal

Each Girls Who Code club aims to use technology to benefit the community. In reading and hearing the news about DACA, we recognized our own lack of knowledge about immigration issues and saw a need to get the word out to our peers.

How It Might Help Others

  • Address what the current general understanding is of a non-documented person’s rights.
  • How best to protect yourself and your family.
  • Identify community resources.
  • Know your rights under the law.
