About Us

Our Goal

Each Girls Who Code club aims to use technology to benefit the community. We recognized in our own lack of knowledge a need for greater information relating to immigration issues.

How It Might Help Others

  • Address what the current general understanding is of a non-documented person’s rights.
  • How best to protect yourself and your family.
  • Identify community resources.
  • Know your rights under the law.
