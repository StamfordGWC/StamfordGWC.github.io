Applying to Connecticut Colleges/Universities

Students may attend state colleges/universities at the in-state rate if:

  1. They are a permanent resident of Connecticut.
  2. They have completed 2 years of high school in Connecticut.
  3. They have graduated from high school or a GED program in Connecticut.
  4. They file an affidavit with the institution of higher education stating the student has filed for legal immigration status or will file an application when eligible to do so.
