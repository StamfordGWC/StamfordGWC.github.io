Important Information for Undocumented Students

You Can Go to College!

Applying to CT Colleges/Universities

Students may attend state colleges/universities at the in-state rate if:

  1. You are a permanent resident of CT
  2. Must complete 2 years of high school in CT
  3. Must graduate from high school or have a GED program in CT
  4. Must file an affidavit with the institution of higher education stating the student has filed for legal immigration status or will file an application when eligible to do so.
